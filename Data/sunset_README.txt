Sunrise/sunset data gathered from tables generated for Narragansett, RI (41o 25'N, 71o 28'W) by the Astronomical Applications Department
of the US Naval Oceanography Portal (http://aa.usno.navy.mil/data/docs/RS_OneYear.php).  NOTE: QCLCD are stored in local
standard time, so sunrise/sunset requests should be -5 hours (5 hours west) of Greenwich time.
